Reading the chart: "Constituent" departments are the 26 cabinet-level ministries. "Directly subordinate" agencies (SAMR, CAC) are ministerial-rank but structured slightly differently. "National bureaus" (NDA) and "public institutions" (SIC) sit under a parent ministry. The CAC has a dual identity — same office, two nameplates: state-side under the State Council, party-side under the Central Cyberspace Affairs Commission.
